About Me

Designing Nightmares Into Sweet Dreams

My name is Ashelic Rose, and I am a multidisciplinary creative passionate about blending storytelling, design, and digital experiences. As a Web Design student, I enjoy creating visuals and interactive designs that combine strong aesthetics with meaningful concepts. My creative style is inspired by dark fantasy, surrealism, and the beauty found in the unconventional. I am drawn to projects that allow me to explore identity, emotion, and atmosphere while pushing the boundaries of traditional design.

Through my work, I have explored digital illustration, branding, 3D design, and web development. Each project is an opportunity for me to experiment, learn new skills, and transform ideas into immersive experiences. I believe great design is more than just how something looks — it is about the feeling it creates. My goal is to continue developing as a creative professional while building work that is unique, memorable, and authentically my own.
